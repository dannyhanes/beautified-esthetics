Meet Your Esthetician

Nicole Hardy

Nicole is the heart of Beautified Esthetics, an all-natural skincare clinic in Cary, North Carolina. She is oncology-trained and experienced with medically compromised skin, proudly caring for cancer patients and their care teams with gentle, safe modalities.

Her approach is educational first: establish healthy, balanced skin, then address your specific concerns at the cellular level. Every treatment features RAYA All-Natural Skincare — developed and manufactured in Los Angeles with the highest-quality ingredients and time-honored techniques.

“SELF care isn’t selfish — it is VITAL!”

When she isn’t in the treatment room, Nicole is spending time outdoors with her husband and three sons, or exploring great food.
